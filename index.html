Who We Are

RProblems is a research and engineering collective dedicated to solving the world's most complex challenges. We aren't a traditional company; we are a mission-driven organization focused on creating groundbreaking, scalable solutions to problems that have long been considered unsolvable. Our work is defined by a relentless pursuit of innovation and a deep commitment to making a lasting positive impact on humanity.

Our Work

AI-Driven Cloud Optimization

Developed a platform that analyzes complex cloud architectures to autonomously reduce costs by over 40% while simultaneously cutting carbon emissions, enabling sustainable scaling for enterprise-level infrastructure.

Industrial IoT Security Fabric

Engineered a zero-trust security platform for large-scale industrial IoT networks, protecting critical infrastructure from cyber-physical threats by providing end-to-end encryption and anomaly detection for millions of endpoints.

Federated Learning for Medical Research

Created a framework that allows multiple hospitals to collaboratively train AI models on sensitive patient data for disease prediction without ever exposing or centralizing the raw data, accelerating research while guaranteeing patient privacy.
